Webpecker's present and future

Webpecker's mission is to provide a user-friendly environment to scrape and store beneficial data from data-rich websites like social networks and Google or Wikipedia.

Currently Webpecker provides scraping usernames, names, and avatars of tweets on a Twitter web page, whether it is the tweets of a searched phrase, hashtag, user, or your homepage or a user's page.

As the Webpecker grows, we will add not only new features, but new target websites like Instagram, Facebook, Wikipedia, etc, each with their own features to scrape all the juicy valuable information.

All the data you scrape are stored locally in your computer and none gets sent to anywhere. You can download them as CSV (XLSX), JSON, and Zip formats or clear them whenever you want. Webpecker is a local software and does not communicate your interactions with it in anyway.
